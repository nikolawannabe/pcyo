Peninsula Community Youth Orchestra is proud to announce we will be partnering with Christine Faught of String Beans Studio from Port Orchard to offer group classes in early music education and violin for very young children starting in January 2026 in both Port Angeles and Sequim. Sprouts classes are for young childen under the age of 6 and will run on Mondays or Fridays in Port Angeles and Sequim. See our calendar page for schedule details for Sprouts classes, when holidays and breaks are, and when terms start and finish.

Christine has 30 years of experience teaching Suzuki classes and private lessons, and directing youth orchestras from New York and Texas. She has recently returned to her roots in Washington. We are so grateful to have her traveling from Port Orchard to Sequim and Port Angeles to offer early music education for our youngest students. Suzuki group classes have not been available in Port Angeles for decades. Christine also offers private lessons to 3–6 year old students, which pair perfectly with her Suzuki Sprouts classes and are in extremely short supply in Port Angeles.

This 50-minute weekly class blends rhythm, song, and playful movement to build the coordination, listening, and focus skills that form the foundation for violin study. Young children sing, tap, move, and explore music through scarves, rhythm instruments, and song stories in a warm, structured setting.

Designed for ages 3–6, the class develops timing, motor control, and musical awareness through familiar songs like Frog in the Meadow and Lightly Row, as well as classical favorites such as Fingal’s Cave and selections from standard Suzuki repertoire. Each session follows a consistent format that balances predictability with discovery - helping children feel confident, attentive, and excited about learning.

Tuition is $150 per term, with $50 due at registration and the remainder due 1 week before class starts.

Peninsula Community Sound Sprouts brings real music—classical, folk, and traditional songs—into children’s lives through play, movement, and family connection. Designed for children from infancy through early childhood, Sound Sprouts helps develop listening, rhythm, coordination, and connection with others while nurturing a lifelong appreciation for great music.

Classes combine singing, rhythm instruments, storytelling, and imaginative movement to masterpieces by Bach, Handel, and other composers. Every activity engages the whole child - body, mind, and spirit - through active listening, singing in tune, and keeping a steady beat.

Parents participate alongside their children, gaining simple tools to make music part of daily life. No musical background is required - just a willingness to sing, move, and connect.

Peninsula Community Sound Sprouts is not entertainment; it’s education made joyful - an invitation to grow together through real music, naturally.

Tuition is $150 per term, with $50 due at registration and the remainder due 1 week before class starts.

Registration opens November 1st. Winter term starts January 9th, 2026.
